About

My name is Collin Hugo and I'm an 18-year-old student. I'm currently doing my first year in BUT MMI (Métiers du Multimédia et de l'Internet) at the IUT du Nord Franche-Comté. I've always had a passion for the world of multimedia, and I came to MMI to develop my skills. For this first year, we have to launch a website project on a film replica. I chose Hagrid's famous line from the first volume of Harry Potter, "You're a wizard, Harry". It's one of the first cult lines from 'Harry Potter and the Philosopher's Stone'. Through my website, I'm going to give users a great experience, immersing them in the magical world of the Harry Potter saga.

This project is of course based on strict constraints. Our site has to be made for a mobile version, in two languages, French and English, to reach an international audience. We also have to stick to a tight deadline. The site has to consist of a home page with several elements, including the main articles, the emblematic line and a short introduction.

We need to consider the typical user for our site, and we need to ensure an optimal experience for our visitors. First and foremost, the site needs to focus on the line "You're a wizard, Harry". My site will therefore include 7 detailed articles on the book, the film, the director, etc... These articles will be accompanied by additional information such as a timeline to show other books or a gallery of films related to the article on the Harry Potter film.

We need to use artificial intelligence to create content in English and for our articles. It's only a help, of course, as we just have to rely on what the AI does. To do this, we need to detail the choice of prompt, the adjustments made, the results and finally what we've changed.

In summary, the aim of this project is to create a website based on a film replica as a starting point. The project has to respect certain rules and constraints in order to optimize our site as much as possible. This is our first project, so it will give us a chance to learn how to work on a big project and to get to grips with several tools.
